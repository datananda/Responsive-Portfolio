About Me

I am a data experience designer currently honing my full-stack web development skills at the University of Minnesota Coding Boot Camp. I believe that data can and should be represented in a way that is both functional and beautiful. My Boot Camp goal is to gain the skills to build advanced and custom web-based visualizations.


I grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah exploring nearby ski resorts and National Parks which spoiled me when it comes to snow and outdoor activities. Now that I live in the flatlands, I find myself spending more time indoors, be it practicing ashtanga yoga, tending my orchid collection, or working towards my secret aspiration of becoming a b-girl (breakdance girl).
